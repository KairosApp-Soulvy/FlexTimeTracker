Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 21, 2026

FlexTime Tracker is designed with your privacy as a core principle. The app operates entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, or share any data whatsoever.

No Data Collection

FlexTime Tracker does not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics. We have no servers and no databases.

Local-Only Storage

All of your time entries, projects, flex bank balances, and settings are stored exclusively on your device. Nothing leaves your iPhone.

No Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms. There are:

  • No analytics or crash reporting tools
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud syncing services

No Tracking

FlexTime Tracker does not track your location, browsing activity, or any other behavior. The app does not use cookies, device identifiers, or fingerprinting of any kind.

Your Data, Your Control

Since all data lives on your device, deleting the app removes all associated data. There is nothing for us to delete on our end because we never had it.
