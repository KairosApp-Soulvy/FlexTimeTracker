## Description

FlexTime Tracker is the simple, powerful way to manage your flex time, overtime, and banked hours — all without giving up a shred of your privacy.

**Built for workers with flex time policies.** Whether you're in government, healthcare, or a corporate environment with use-it-or-lose-it flex time, FlexTime Tracker helps you stay on top of your hours so you never lose banked time.

**Clock In & Out with One Tap**
Start and stop your work timer instantly. Track hours across multiple projects and see exactly where your time goes each day and week.

**Flex Banking with Expiration Tracking**
Bank your overtime hours and watch your flex balance grow. FlexTime Tracker tracks the 90-day (or custom) expiration window so you know exactly when banked hours expire — and can use them before they're gone.

**Configurable Policies**
Every workplace is different. Set your own standard hours, flex expiration period, overtime rules, and banking policies to match your employer's system.

**100% Private — Your Data Never Leaves Your Device**
No accounts. No cloud. No analytics. No tracking. Everything stays on your iPhone. Period.

• One-tap clock in/out
• Project-based time tracking
• Flex bank with expiration alerts
• Weekly and daily hour breakdowns
• Configurable work policies
• Completely offline and private
