FlexTime Tracker — Help & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I clock in and out?

From the Today tab, tap the large clock-in button to start tracking your time. When you're done, tap the button again to clock out. Your hours are automatically calculated and recorded.

You can also assign your time to a specific project before clocking in.

How does flex banking work?

When you work more than your standard hours (e.g., more than 8 hours in a day), the extra time is automatically banked as flex time. You can view your total banked balance in the Balance tab.

Banked flex hours have an expiration date based on your configured policy (default: 90 days). The app shows you when each batch of banked hours will expire so you can plan to use them.

How do I set the flex expiration policy?

Go to Settings and look for Flex Policy. Here you can configure:

  • Standard hours per day — your regular workday length
  • Expiration period — how many days banked hours last (e.g., 90 days)
  • Overtime rules — how extra hours are calculated

Set these to match your employer's flex time policy.

Is my data backed up?

FlexTime Tracker stores all data locally on your device. Your data is included in your iPhone's standard iCloud or iTunes backups. We do not have access to any of your data — it never leaves your device.

What happens if I delete the app?

Deleting the app removes all stored data from your device. Since there is no cloud backup, this cannot be undone. Make sure to export any data you need before uninstalling.
